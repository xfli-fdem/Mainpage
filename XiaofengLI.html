Biography

Hello, welcome to my personal webpage!!! My name is Xiaofeng Li and I am a researcher working at the University of Toronto. I obtained my PhD degree from Institute of Rock and Soil Mechanics, Chinese Academy of Sciences (also University of Chinese Academy of Sciences) and Monash University , supervised by Prof. Haibo Li and Prof. Jian Zhao. Then, I did postdoctoral research at PolyU from 2020-2021. Currently, I am a postdoc in Grasselli's Geomechanics Group at the University of Toronto.

I am skilled at C++, C, JavaScript, Matlab, HTML and R programing, and be familiar with Python and CUDA.
